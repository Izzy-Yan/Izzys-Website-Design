My Process

Clear steps, transparent communication, and a smooth experience from start to finish.

How It Works

1. Request a Quote

You fill out a short form describing your project. I review it and send a quote range and next steps.

2. Project Details & Planning

You complete the project intake form with pages, content, branding, and goals. Scope and timeline are confirmed.

3. First Draft

I design and build the first full version of your website. Typical timeline: 5–14 days depending on size.

4. Revisions

You review the site and submit feedback. One revision round is included.

5. Final Delivery & Launch

Final changes are applied, mobile optimization is completed, and the site is launched.

Edits & Revisions Policy

Any changes requested after the included revision round or after launch are billed separately:

  • Small text/image edits: $25
  • Design or layout changes: $50/hour
  • New pages or features quoted separately
